## Summary

An `AttesterSlashing` is slashable under the SURROUND-VOTE predicate when one attestation surrounds the other's FFG vote: `a.source.epoch < b.source.epoch AND a.target.epoch > b.target.epoch`. Either direction counts (check both `(a,b)` and `(b,a)`). Combined with double-vote (DSL-014), these are the two attester-slashable conditions in Ethereum/DIG consensus.

---

## Specification

Surround-vote predicate:
```text
(a.source.epoch <  b.source.epoch AND a.target.epoch >  b.target.epoch)
OR
(b.source.epoch <  a.source.epoch AND b.target.epoch >  a.target.epoch)
```

Full verification pipeline identical to DSL-014 except the predicate in step 3 is this one.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `a` surrounds `b` → accepted.
- [ ] `b` surrounds `a` → accepted (mirror).
- [ ] Non-overlapping epoch windows → not accepted via DSL-015 path.
- [ ] Equal source or equal target → not surround-vote.
- [ ] Combined with DSL-014 predicate, every slashable case is accepted; every non-slashable case is rejected as `AttesterSlashingNotSlashable` (DSL-017).
