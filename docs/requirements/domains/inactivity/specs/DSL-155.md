# DSL-155: InactivityScoreTracker::rewind_on_reorg

> **Authoritative:** [DSL-155](../NORMATIVE.md#DSL-155) | **Verification:** [VERIFICATION.md](../VERIFICATION.md) | **Tracking:** [TRACKING.yaml](../TRACKING.yaml) | **SPEC:** §9.2, §13

---

## Summary

`InactivityScoreTracker::rewind_on_reorg(depth)` restores per-validator scores from an internal ring-buffer of prior-epoch snapshots. Drops `depth` epochs of accumulated increments. Depth bounded by `CORRELATION_WINDOW_EPOCHS` (36) — reorgs past the ring-buffer trigger `ReorgTooDeep` at the orchestrator (DSL-130).

---

## Specification

impl InactivityScoreTracker {

}

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Scores restored to snapshot at `current_epoch - depth`.
- [ ] Ring-buffer depth of `CORRELATION_WINDOW_EPOCHS` (36).
- [ ] `depth == 0` is a no-op.
- [ ] Vec length unchanged (resize handled separately via `resize_for`).
