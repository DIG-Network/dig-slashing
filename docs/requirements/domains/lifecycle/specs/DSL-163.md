# DSL-163: SlashingResult + PerValidatorSlash + FinalisationResult Serde Roundtrip

> **Authoritative:** [DSL-163](../NORMATIVE.md#DSL-163) | **Verification:** [VERIFICATION.md](../VERIFICATION.md) | **Tracking:** [TRACKING.yaml](../TRACKING.yaml) | **SPEC:** §3.9, §18

---

## Summary

`SlashingResult`, `PerValidatorSlash`, and `FinalisationResult` MUST round-trip byte-exactly via bincode (and serde_json for RPC/telemetry). Required for snapshot/restore, RPC responses, and test fixtures.

---

## Specification

All three types `derive(Serialize, Deserialize, PartialEq, Eq)`.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `SlashingResult` with populated `per_validator` vec round-trips.
- [ ] `PerValidatorSlash` individual round-trips.
- [ ] `FinalisationResult` with `per_validator_correlation_penalty: Vec<(u32, u64)>` round-trips.
- [ ] Zero-valued fields preserved.
