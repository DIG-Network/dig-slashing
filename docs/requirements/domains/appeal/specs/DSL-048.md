# DSL-048: Attester Appeal Rejected on Genuine Slash

> **Authoritative:** [DSL-048](../NORMATIVE.md#DSL-048) | **Verification:** [VERIFICATION.md](../VERIFICATION.md) | **Tracking:** [TRACKING.yaml](../TRACKING.yaml) | **SPEC:** §6.3, §22.5

---

## Summary

An `AttesterSlashingAppeal` whose ground does NOT hold against a genuine attester slashing MUST be Rejected. Covers every ground variant: identical/non-slashable/empty/sig-invalid/structure-invalid/not-in-intersection when the evidence is genuinely slashable.

---

## Specification

Each appeal ground check is a positive predicate. Any ground whose predicate fails on the evidence → `AppealVerdict::Rejected`.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Genuine double-vote; `AttestationsIdentical` ground → Rejected.
- [ ] Genuine surround-vote; `NotSlashableByPredicate` ground → Rejected.
- [ ] Non-empty intersection; `EmptyIntersection` ground → Rejected.
- [ ] Valid aggregate sigs; `SignatureAInvalid`/`SignatureBInvalid` grounds → Rejected.
- [ ] Well-formed structure; `InvalidIndexedAttestationStructure` ground → Rejected.
- [ ] Index IS in intersection; `ValidatorNotInIntersection` ground → Rejected.
